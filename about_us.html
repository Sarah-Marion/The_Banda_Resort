About Us


Offering the perfect fusion of European luxury and Kenyan hospitality, The Banda Resort is positioned between the city and the lakeside. The resort is a unique destination where guests can spend time relaxing or working, and in addition to the 80 rooms and suites distributed throughout its open grounds, you will also find exquisite dining opportunities here.

In addition to our all-day dining restaurant, Cafe Tree, you can try a range of menus and dining styles across the lounge; Sugar Mill Bar; The Library; and our Levant -style Lake Cabanas and Concierge Services.
